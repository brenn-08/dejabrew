Welcome to Deja Brew: A Cozy New Cafe in Baguio!

Est. 2024

Nestled in the heart of Baguio, Deja Brew is your new go-to destination for a delightful coffee experience. With a warm and inviting atmosphere, our cafe is designed for everyone—from coffee enthusiasts to those simply looking for a cozy spot to unwind. We proudly serve a curated selection of freshly brewed coffee, artisanal teas, and delectable pastries, all made with love and the finest ingredients.

At Deja Brew, we believe that every sip should evoke a sense of nostalgia and comfort, reminiscent of the rich café culture that Baguio is known for. Our passionate baristas are dedicated to crafting the perfect cup, whether you prefer a classic espresso or a unique seasonal blend.

Join us in our beautifully designed space, where you can catch up with friends, enjoy a quiet moment alone, or immerse yourself in the vibrant local community. Come and experience the charm of Deja Brew—where every visit feels like a warm hug in a cup!

Daily Specials at Deja Brew

1. Coffee of the Day: A rotating selection of our finest brews, highlighting unique flavors and origins. Ask your barista for today’s special!
2. Sweet Treat Thursdays: Enjoy any pastry with your drink for just $2! Choose from our freshly baked croissants, muffins, or cookies.
3. Weekend Brunch Special: Join us every Saturday and Sunday for our Weekend Brunch! Enjoy a complimentary coffee with any brunch item, featuring:
  • Eggs Benedict - Poached eggs, Canadian bacon, and hollandaise sauce on a toasted English muffin.
  • Breakfast Burrito - Scrambled eggs, cheese, and your choice of sausage or veggies wrapped in a warm tortilla.
4. Seasonal Flavor Showcase: Experience our limited-time seasonal flavors, such as:
  • Pumpkin Spice Latte - A fall favorite with rich pumpkin, spices, and whipped cream.
  • Peppermint Mocha - A holiday treat combining chocolate, espresso, and a refreshing mint twist.
5. Happy Hour: Join us from 3 PM to 5 PM for Happy Hour! Enjoy 20% off all beverages and select snacks. Perfect for a mid-afternoon pick-me-up!
6. Community Thursdays: Every Thursday, bring a friend and enjoy a buy-one-get-one-free on any coffee or tea. Connect with friends while sipping your favorites!
7. Healthier Choice: Try our Quinoa Salad Bowl— a nutritious blend of quinoa, fresh vegetables, and a zesty dressing, perfect for a light lunch!
